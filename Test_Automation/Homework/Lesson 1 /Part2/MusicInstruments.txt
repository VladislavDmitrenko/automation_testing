Class "Music instruments"
	Attributes:
name, country of origin, instrument group (wind instruments, electro-musical instruments,
keyboards, stringed instruments, percussion instruments), type of register (high, medium, low),
size, weight, height, color, case material (metal, wood, leather),
instrument components (neck, deck, strings, keys, plates),
the presence of holes (yes, no), the degree of opening (up, down),
belonging to the orchestra (chamber, symphonic).
	Methods:
play, not play, sound, not sound, creak, whistle, roar, string vibrations, bloated sounds, filling bits.
